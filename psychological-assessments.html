Comprehensive Psychological Assessments

We provide comprehensive assessments for children, adolescents and adults.

Each assessment is based on the individual's needs and may include:

  • Autism Spectrum Disorder
  • Specific Learning Disorder (Dyslexia, Dysgraphia, Dyscalculia)
  • Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder
  • Eating Disorders
  • Intellectual Disability
  • Giftedness
  • Behaviours
  • Trauma
  • Depression
  • Anxiety

Our Tools

Our assessment batteries have been developed in line with best practice principles and are extremely thorough. These include (but are not limited to):

WISC-V A&NZ (Wechsler Intelligence Scale for Children - 5th Edition)

WIAT-III A&NZ (Wechsler Individual Achievement Test - 3rd Edition)

WAIS-IV A&NZ (Wechsler Adult Intelligence Scale - 4th Edition)

WPPSI-IV A&NZ (Wechsler Preschool and Primary Scale of Intelligence - 4th Edition)

SB-5 (Stanford Binet - 5th Edition)

MIGDAS-2 (Monteiro Interview Guidelines for Diagnosing the Autism Spectrum - 2nd Edition)

ADI-R (Autism Diagnostic Interview - Revised Edition)

GARS-3 (Gilliam Autism Rating Scale - 3rd Edition)

ASSQ (Autism Spectrum Screening Questionnaire) CAT-Q (The Camouflaging Autistic Traits Questionnaire)

ABAS-III (Adaptive Behaviour Assessment System - 3rd Edition)

TOVA 9 (Test of Variables of Attention - 9th Edition)

Young DIVA-5 (Diagnostic Interview for ADHD in Children - 5th Edition)

DIVA-5 (Diagnostic Interview for ADHD in Adults - 5th Edition)

Conners 4 (Conners 4th Edition)

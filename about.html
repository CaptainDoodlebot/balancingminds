ABOUT CASSANDRA

Cassandra Gist is the Director of Balancing Minds Psychology. Cassandra is endorsed as a Health Psychologist, with substantial experience working with children, adolescents and adults and has a specific interest in psychological assessments. Aside from private practice, Cassandra has worked in hospitals and private schools, integrating her skills across many communities in Queensland, NSW and Victoria.

She has successfully completed a Bachelor Degree in Psychology (Hons); a Master's Degree in Psychology and has attained endorsement as a Health Psychologist. Cassandra is a registered member of the Australian Psychological Society (APS), a member of the governing body, Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (AHPRA), and a Member of Australian ADHD Professional Association (AADPA).

Cassandra uses the latest technology within her practice and is able to undertake assessments and therapy online using the latest Telehealth portals.

Cassandra has her own family, complete with two active dogs who assist with her life balance and wellbeing. She actively engages in Yoga classes and is often found at the beach walking her dogs. Cassandra's caring nature and slight humor attributes to her holistic practice to ensure smooth rapport building and consistency of therapy.

Cassandra is introducing a therapy dog to her practice - meet Louie! He will be seen on Telehealth until he is fully trained.
